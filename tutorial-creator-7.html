How to Use

Recording Mode:

  1. Enter App URL - Type the URL of the web app you want to create a tutorial for
  2. Load App - Click "Load App" to load the app in the iframe
  3. Start Recording - Click "⏺ Start Recording" when you're ready to begin the tutorial
  4. Interact Normally - Use your app normally. All interactions are being captured via the API!
  5. Pause Recording - Click "⏸ Pause" to pause and add annotations or review your work
  6. Add Annotations - While paused:
    • Click "➕ Add Annotation"
    • Enter title, text, and duration
    • Click "✨ Create Annotation"
    • Drag the annotation box to position it exactly where you want
    • Click "Save" when ready
  7. Preview While Recording - While paused, click "👁 Preview" to see your work so far, then click "↩️ Resume Recording" to continue
  8. Resume Recording - Click "▶ Resume" to continue recording
  9. Stop Recording - Click "⏹ Stop Recording" when done
  10. Preview Final - Click "👁 Preview" to watch the complete replay with annotations
  11. Export - Click "💾 Export Tutorial" to get the JSON, then copy it to save

Timeline:

  • Blue markers - Single events (simulate, parameter changes, etc.)
  • Purple bars - Drag operations (from mouse down to mouse up)
  • Cyan bars - Annotations (text overlays during playback)
